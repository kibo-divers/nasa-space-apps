In an alternate universe, Earth #67, the Space Race ended too late. Millions upon millions of debris in orbit have enveloped the Earth completely.

The Hubble Space Telescope was destroyed by space debris. However, the James Webb Space Telescope and several ground telescopes discovered the near-Earth asteroid "Impactor-2025."

It is practically impossible to send any sort of spacecraft outside of our atmosphere. There is no chance to divert any incoming asteroids from colliding with our home planet.

The only way to prolong civilization is to keep as many people away from the impact area.
